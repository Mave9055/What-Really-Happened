# WRH Outcomes and Measures

## Purpose

Define core pilot measurement categories used to evaluate WRH deployment feasibility and participant impact.

## Recommended Minimum Metrics

- Pre/post stress regulation awareness self-report
- Participant retention and attendance rate
- Qualitative reflection themes
- Facilitator protocol adherence notes

## Reporting Cadence

- Baseline at intake
- Weekly tracking during delivery
- Post-program summary at integration
