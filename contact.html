The Pilot Program

The Johnson County pilot proposal represents the first implementation site for the curriculum. A pilot program runs for 16 weeks with 90-minute weekly sessions, full documentation, and case study output.

The pilot is designed to:

  • Establish the curriculum as deployable and clinically effective
  • Generate outcome data and participant feedback
  • Provide facilitator training and certification
  • Build a replicable model for other institutions
