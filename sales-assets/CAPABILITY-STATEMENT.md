# CAPABILITY STATEMENT: Capitol Contracts LLC

**The Revolution is Trauma-Informed.**

## Core Competencies
Capitol Contracts LLC is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) specializing in the development and delivery of trauma-literacy curricula. Our flagship program, **"What Really Happened: A Trauma Education Series,"** is a psychoeducational group curriculum designed for institutions that require immediate, high-impact trauma education.

### Key Offerings:
- **18-Session Case Study Curriculum:** A structured, 18-session sequence grounded in a documented life.
- **Facilitator-Ready Toolkits:** Word-for-word scripts, stage directions, and clinical appendices requiring zero prior clinical training for delivery.
- **Somatic-Minimalist Design:** Materials engineered to minimize nervous system dysregulation during the learning process.
- **Deployment-Ready Frameworks:** Tailored for VA Medical Centers, Residential Treatment Programs, and Community Support Groups.

## Past Performance & Differentiators
- **Lived-Experience Methodology:** Unlike traditional academic curricula, our program uses a single, sustained case study to bridge the gap between clinical theory and human reality.
- **Veteran-Centric:** Developed by a Veteran for Veterans, specifically addressing the "unreachable" demographic in clinical settings.
- **Low-Barrier Facilitation:** Designed to be deployed by peer support specialists, chaplains, or social workers, reducing the burden on clinical staff.
