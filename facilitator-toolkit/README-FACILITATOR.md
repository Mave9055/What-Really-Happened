# CPTSD Course Curriculum

This repository contains a full sequence of lectures and supporting documents designed to navigate the complexities of Complex Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (CPTSD). These are not just academic notes; they are a blueprint for survival and understanding the reality of trauma.

## Course Structure
The curriculum is organized into 17 sessions, moving from initial safety to the mechanics of healing:

* **Foundations:** Welcome, Safety, and the Blueprint of Survival.
* **The Reality of Trauma:** Exploring "masks," the war on reality, and the breaking point.
* **The Healing Process:** Understanding what recovery actually looks like and navigating relapses.

## Included Files
* **Master Course Document:** The primary guide for the curriculum.
* **Clinical Peer Review Letter:** Professional context for the materials.
* **Sessions 00-16:** Step-by-step lecture documents.
