# Session 01 Participant Handout (Sample)

## Overview

This handout supports participant retention after Session 01.

## Who it is for

WRH participants.

## How to use

Read after session and complete the quick reflection prompt.

## Key reminders

- Your reactions were learned under stress.
- Freeze/fawn/shutdown are survival responses.
- Noticing is progress.

## One-minute check-in

- What state was strongest for me this week? (overwhelm / shutdown / grounded)
- What helped me return to center?

## Limits and disclaimers

This handout is educational and not emergency mental health guidance.
