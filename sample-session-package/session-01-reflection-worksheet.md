# Session 01 Reflection Worksheet (Sample)

## Overview

Simple worksheet to consolidate learning without forced disclosure.

## Who it is for

WRH participants.

## How to use

Participants may complete privately during or after session.

## Reflection prompts

1. What did I recognize about my stress responses today?
2. Which reframe felt most true for me?
3. One small regulation action I will try this week:
4. One support I may need:

## Limits and disclaimers

Participants can skip any prompt. This worksheet is not a clinical assessment tool.
