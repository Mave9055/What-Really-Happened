# What Really Happened: A Trauma Education Series

A complete, deployable trauma education curriculum built from one documented life. 18 sessions. Word-for-word facilitator scripts. No clinical training required to facilitate.

## About

**What Really Happened** is a psychoeducational group curriculum designed for institutions that need trauma literacy now. It teaches the mechanisms of Complex PTSD through the sustained examination of one documented life, using a case study methodology where every clinical concept is grounded in specific events, relationships, and moments.

The curriculum is not a wellness brand. It is not a therapy program. It is a serious, clinical, human document about what Complex PTSD actually looks like from inside the nervous system that lives it.

## The Curriculum

- **18 complete sessions** organized in five parts
- **Word-for-word facilitator scripts** with stage directions and timing
- **Clinical appendices** and discussion prompts for each session
- **DEEP DIVE expansions** for clinician-level groups
- **Safety protocol** (Session 12) for crisis and suicide risk
- **Deployable from day one** — no clinical training required to facilitate

### The Five Parts

1. **Part I: How It Gets Built Into You** — Early development and trauma formation
2. **Part II: How It Destroys Your Relationships** — Adult relationships and relational consequences
3. **Part III: How You Tried to Cope** — Coping strategies and regulation mechanisms
4. **Part IV: The Internal Battlefield** — Crisis architecture and neuroscience
5. **Part V: Changing the World Around You** — Integration and systemic change

## Deployment Contexts

The curriculum is designed for five primary deployment contexts:

- **Recovery & Residential Treatment Programs** — Addresses addiction as regulatory strategy, not moral failure
- **Correctional & Reentry Settings** — Teaches neuroscience of why the brain underwater cannot execute compliance
- **Community Mental Health & Social Services** — Plain language, no jargon, no diagnostic gatekeeping
- **Clinician & Professional Training** — Trauma literacy for any setting where people are expected to function
- **Workforce & Peer Support Programs** — Employer wellness, peer support specialist training, community education

## Deployment

This site is ready for GitHub Pages deployment from the `main` branch, root directory.

1. Push all files to GitHub
2. Enable GitHub Pages in repository settings
3. Select `main` branch, `/ (root)` as source
4. Site will be live at `https://mave9055.github.io/What-Really-Happened/`

The `<base href="/What-Really-Happened/">` tag in every HTML file ensures all links work correctly under the GitHub Pages subdirectory.
