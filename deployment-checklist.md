# WRH Deployment Checklist

## Overview

Use this checklist before launching a WRH cohort.

## Who it is for

Site leads, facilitators, and operations staff.

## How to use

Mark each item complete before Session 00.

---

## Governance and staffing

- [ ] Program owner assigned
- [ ] Lead facilitator assigned
- [ ] Clinical supervisor/consult pathway confirmed
- [ ] Co-facilitator or support role identified

## Safety and escalation

- [ ] Local crisis pathways documented (988 + local resources)
- [ ] Emergency escalation chain tested
- [ ] Mandated reporting policy reviewed with team
- [ ] `SAFETY_AND_ESCALATION.md` reviewed by facilitators

## Participant workflow

- [ ] Intake criteria finalized
- [ ] Exclusion/defer criteria documented
- [ ] Consent and participation expectations approved
- [ ] Attendance and outcomes tracking method configured

## Curriculum and materials

- [ ] `facilitator-manual.md` reviewed end-to-end
- [ ] Session 00 materials prepared
- [ ] Handouts printed/shared
- [ ] `sample-session-package/` reviewed in facilitator prep

## Accessibility and inclusion

- [ ] Language access plan in place
- [ ] Captioning/accessibility tools configured
- [ ] Low-literacy alternatives ready
- [ ] Neurodivergent accommodation options planned

## Pilot and quality assurance

- [ ] Pilot cohort schedule approved
- [ ] Debrief cadence scheduled (weekly minimum)
- [ ] Incident documentation template available
- [ ] Improvement loop owner assigned

## Limits and disclaimers

WRH should not launch without defined crisis and clinical integration pathways.
